####

#### The McCumber Cube  A professional framework used to manage information security. It consists of three critical dimensions:

1. Security Principles: Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (CIA Triad).
2. Data States: Transmission, Storage, and Processing.
3. Safeguards: Technology, Policies/Practices, and People (Awareness).\

#### Threat Actors & Motivations

* White Hat Hackers: Ethical hackers using their skills for defensive purposes.
* Black Hat Hackers: Malicious actors seeking personal gain or causing damage.
* Grey Hat Hackers: Individuals who may bypass laws without malicious intent.
* State-Sponsored Actors: Highly sophisticated groups performing espionage or infrastructure sabotage (e.g., Stuxnet).
